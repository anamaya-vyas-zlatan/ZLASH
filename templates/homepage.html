Welcome to ZLASH



Being an entrepreneur or a kidpreneur can put you at difficult positions sometimes. You know you have a product (prototype) that could be worth millions but what you lack is resources- money, investors, strategic partners, etc. ZLASH helps you connect to the investors around who are looking for making investments in growing companies, or prototypes or products they see potential in. Sometimes, it happens that you as an entrepreneur have all the resources- manufacturing centers and money to put in your business but don't have a product or any idea that could help people. In this case, you can have a look at the problems posted by people and if you can find a solution to that, you very well have your own company on the way!

Start Growing


As an investor, you might be looking for potential businneses to invest in, or maybe in our future generation- the kidpreneurs who have a great idea and a great product but not enough money to grow. With your help, these products can earn a great amount and be just the right investment for you. ZLASH helps you connect to different start-ups all around. You can view the details about their business and look at their CVs and contact them if interested. All the deals to be made externally only.

Hopefully someone will see to your problem and find a solution and will be able to build-up their company around it.
